How It Works
What are Unicode watermarks?
Unicode has multiple "space" characters that look identical but have different codes. We replace normal spaces (U+0020) with AI-specific variants when you copy text.
Which AIs are supported?
ChatGPT (U+2009), Claude (U+200A), Gemini (U+2005), Copilot (U+2006), Perplexity (U+2007), Poe (U+2004), and more.
Can watermarks be removed?
Yes, by pasting into Notepad or running text normalization. But they survive in Google Docs, Word, email, and social media.
Is 100% watermarked always AI?
Only if our extension was installed! This detects watermarks WE inject, not all AI text.
